## How to read this

- **MASE < 1** means the model beats a seasonal-naive baseline on average absolute error.
- **sMAPE** is in percent, range 0-200; lower is better.
- **Coverage@80** is a single-holdout sanity check — well-calibrated intervals land near 0.80. Models without native quantile output show `nan`.
- **seconds** is the total wall-clock time for all folds, including fit + predict.

## Notes

- The airline series is short (144 obs) and strongly seasonal — a setting where classical methods (ARIMA, Holt-Winters) have a structural advantage over ML/foundation models that rely on larger context windows. Don't read these numbers as a global ranking; they're a sanity check that the unified API works end-to-end.
- Running this benchmark on your own data is the right thing to do. The repo ships `scripts/benchmark.py` as a template — point it at `load_csv(...)` with your series and the protocol above will apply verbatim.
